What is Splatoon?

Splatoon is a third-person shooter that has multiple modes, the most popular mode being Turf War. Instead of shooting bullets like in regular games, the weapons you use shoot ink! Each mode consists of 2 teams of 4 players. I forgot to mention, that your character can be selected as an inkling or octoling. Why is this important? Because your character can turn into a squid or an octopus and swim through your color of ink!!!

Turf War

In this mode, Both teams compete and try to cover as much of the map as they can in under 3 minutes. Yeah this one's pretty simple but the other modes are listed below:

Clam Blitz

There are clams scattered around the map and players must collect them. Once a player has enough (usually 8), the clams will combine into a football. Also, there is a hanging basket in the enemies base which can only be opened with a football. Once the shield is broken, regular clams can be thrown in. The game ends if a teams basket is filled to the max with clams or whoever basket has the most. Clams will be dropped if you die.

Rainmaker

This weapon is set in the middle of the stage and the players need to shoot at the barrier around it to get it! The players then need to bring the rainmaker to pedal stones to win!

Tower control

Similar to rainmaker, a structure is set in the middle of the stage and the tower will move when a player is on it (direction goes to the enemy base). The tower will travel around the stage and will make different stops. First to the finish line wins! or the farthest the tower moves if you run out of time.

Splat Zones

A zone in the middle of the stage is highlighted and players must cover the area with their color of ink to claim it. A team must keep the zone for 99 seconds in order to win. Time is added to your count if the enemy claims it while you have it.

Strategies (Important!)

Just to note that the clips that were suppose to be here didn't work out so I'm just gonna list the stratgies

Turf War

In turf war, you need to be quick about spreading your ink. The sooner you get to the center of the map, the more area you can cover. staying in your base and slowly making your way to the middle will give the enemy more time to claim more area and then from that point, it just goes back and forth between the 2 teams. To clarify, get to the middle as quickly as you can to get more area, then when you get splated, ink your side!

Rainmaker

In this mode, the rainmaker has a defense system where a barrier is placed around it (Shoot at it to make it explode!). The best strategy is to have 2 of the four players go ahead of the person holding the rainmaker to try and eliminate players of the opposite team. One person should stay with the person who has the rainmaker to make sure no one comes from behind.

Tower Control

In tower control, At least one person needs to be on the rainmaker while the others try and defend the moving tower. Similar to the strategy of rainmaker, at least 1 person needs to go ahead and eliminate the enemy players.

Splat Zones

The best way to win a Splat Zone match is to kill all of the enemy players before you claim it. it is important to have a weapon that spreads ink quickly in order to claim the zone.

Clam Blitz

This mode is all about sharing. If you see a teammate that has more clams than you, it would be best to throw him some of yours so they can get a football faster. Remember, the football formed is the only way to break the enemies' shield of their basket.
